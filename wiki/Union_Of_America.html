Formation and Development

Path to Independence

The Union of America emerged as the Democratic Party's response to the collapse of the United States federal system in 2025.

November 2024

Election Disputes Begin

Contested presidential election results lead to multiple states refusing to certify results.

January 2025

Constitutional Crisis

Inability to reach consensus on election results triggers constitutional crisis and competing claims to presidency.

February 2025

Democratic Coalition Forms

Democratic governors and state legislatures form coalition to maintain constitutional order.

December 27, 2025

California Breaks Away

2nd California Republic declares independence, triggering nationwide succession crisis.

December 28, 2025

Union of America Forms

Democratic states declare independence as Union of America, escalating the constitutional crisis.

December 29, 2025

Southern Independence Declaration

USSA and Hopi Nation simultaneously declare independence, claiming constitutional legitimacy and sovereignty rights.

November 30, 2025

Republican Union Formation

Trump flees to Midwest US and forms Republican Union of America as conservative counter-government.

December 30, 2025

Regional Breakaways

Hawaii, Texas, New England, and Cascadia declare independence in rapid succession.

December 31, 2025

Federal Collapse

Alaska's social order collapses into anarchy, US Navy flees to territories. United States officially ceases to exist.

January 1, 2026

Second American Civil War Begins

With federal authority completely collapsed, the Second American Civil War officially commences between successor states.

Democratic Continuity

The Union of America positions itself as the legitimate heir to American democratic traditions:

  • Preservation of the U.S. Constitution and Bill of Rights
  • Maintenance of democratic elections and peaceful transitions
  • Protection of civil liberties and minority rights
  • Commitment to international law and treaties
